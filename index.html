About Me

Data Analyst | Turning Numbers into Clear, Actionable Insights

I’ve spent over seven years helping businesses make sense of their data. My job is to take messy, complex information and turn it into clear, actionable insights that teams can use to make better decisions. Whether it’s spotting trends, building easy-to-use dashboards, or finding answers to tough questions, I make data work for people.

What I Do

  • Clean and organize data to make it ready for analysis
  • Create dashboards and reports that are simple to understand
  • Find patterns and trends that help businesses grow
  • Answer key questions using data, like "What’s driving sales?" or "Where can we improve?"

Keeping Data Safe

I take data security seriously. In every project, I ensure data is handled responsibly by:

  • Setting up clear rules for how data is collected, stored, and used
  • Making sure only the right people have access to sensitive information
  • Regularly checking systems to prevent errors or breaches
  • Helping teams understand why data protection matters

How I Work

Making Data Clear

I create dashboards and reports that are easy to read and use. Whether it’s in Power BI, Tableau, or Excel, I focus on making sure everyone—from managers to team members—can understand the story the data is telling.

Solving Problems with Data

I dig into data to find answers to specific questions. For example, I might analyze sales data to figure out why certain products perform better or look at customer feedback to find areas for improvement.

Keeping Data Organized

I set up systems to make sure data is accurate, up-to-date, and easy to access. This means creating processes to clean and organize data so it’s always ready for analysis.

At the end of the day, my goal is simple: to make data useful. I love seeing teams use the insights I provide to make smarter decisions and grow their business.
